About me

I am a final year CS undergrad at VIT Vellore. I currently work at BYJU's where I design conversational UIs to drive user engagement. Formerly, I have interned at Snapchat, where I carried out various usability and validation testing and helped with localization and marketing by creating AR experiences. I got to work collaboratively with the India, Indonesia and LA offices.

Being from an engineering background, I'm aware of how digital products work. But the question that intrigued me the most was who is humanizing this tech? I started with designing posters and logos in my first year, later I found the sweet spot of UX that allowed me to work at the intersection of tech, design and business.

Although I was born in 2000 but I am 90s kid at heart. I enjoy binge watching anime. I am heavily influenced by sneaker culture and Indian hip-hop, every sneaker and rap has a great story behind it and I try taking insights from that
